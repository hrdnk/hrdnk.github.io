this is us and what we do

We are Cooco, printing manufacture in Ukraine specialized in small and medium runs of books, catalogues, magazines, brochures and booklets since 1999. As we combine multiple technologies we can guarantee the best price & excellent result.

PREPRESS

Our repro and prepress technicians check all jobs print to ISO standard, help to correct mistakes in the artwork and prepare it for printing to guarantee high quality of final product. Experience of each repro and prepress technician is over 10 years in the field. Among all services we make professional layouts for books from Word or Pages. So either you have professional artwork or no, we can be helpful anyway

PRODUCTS

Books hard/soft cover, catalogues, magazines, brochures and booklets. Great variety of products and postpress technologies, fast printing and quality control. Our products respond to the high level of the quality requirements as we perform a rigorous checking procedure at every stage of the operation, moreover we are the only company that checks each printed copy.

EQUIPMENT & TECHNOLOGIES

Modern equipment, great stuff and more than 19 years of experience in Ukraine, central Europe, UK, USA. Here is a short list of our equipment: HP Indigo 5500, Ricoh C7200x, Heidelberg SM-74, Heidelberg MOFH, KBA Performa 66, plus full range of post-printing equipment with a number of finishing techniques that creates endless possibilities if combined. Approximate capacity: from 1 to 50000 copies per run
